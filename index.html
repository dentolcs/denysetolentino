About Me

My name is Denyse and during my last year of undergrad, I took a couple of computer science classes and I discovered that I really enjoyed the problem solving aspect, the wide variety of applications it has in the real world, and that there’s always something new to learn. That experience led me to enroll in Oregon State’s post-bacc program in computer science. The classes I've taken and the projects I've done so far has really affirmed my decision to go down this path, so here I am now!

On a more personal note, I love reading books that invoke deep feelings which, for me, typically lead to sad and tragic stories. Occasionally, I do enjoy reading less intense books. As motivation to read more and heal my attention span, I'm sharing my thoughts on some books I've read on here as well.
